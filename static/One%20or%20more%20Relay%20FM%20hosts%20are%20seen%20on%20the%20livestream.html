Game round: 3Host: JamesPick: 4.2Score: 0

  • There is some debate about the rules because a few Relay FM hosts will be in attendance, including both John and Jason. By the letter of the Charter, hosts aren't allowed to try to induce their own picks to come true, but there is no restriction on other hosts trying to make another pick be false. Thus, John and Jason will want to avoid the cameras to deny James the points and that is allowed, but James is friends with other Relay FM hosts and could try to illegally induce the pick remotely. Eventually, some ground rules are established:
    • James may not ask any Relay host to do anything unusual to appear on camera.
    • John and Jason are allowed to try to stay off the live stream, but may not break any Apple Park rules to do so. They are also not allowed to interfere with the visibility of other Relay FM hosts.
    • Dad Stephen Hackett may be called in by any host to adjudicate whether anyone is, quote, "up to some shenanigans".
