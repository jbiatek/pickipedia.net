The Pickies Charter (2021)

jbiatek22nd December 2022 at 1:14am
  1. There are two types of Pickies: Annual Pickies and WWDC Pickies. No other Pickies shall ever be introduced.
  2. The winner of the Annual Pickies is named Grand Admiral Pickerton, and retains the title for the full year.
  3. The WWDC Pickies winner is named the San Jose Shindig King, and retains the title until the next WWDC is held.
  4. Order for Annual Pickies and WWDC Pickies is based on the winner of the previous year.
  5. The winner goes second. The losers flip a coin to determine who goes first and who goes third. Jason is the only person who is allowed to flip a coin, and cannot use any apps to do so.
  6. To earn any points, everything written down in the prediction document must come true.
  7. There are no half points awarded in any round, but other fractions are allowed.
  8. Three points are awarded for any pick deemed correct in the first round.
  9. Two points are awarded for any pick deemed correct in the second round.
  10. One point will be awarded for correct picks in the lightning round.
  11. Any pick that comes true in the first half of the year is worth double.
  12. Your total percentage (expressed as a fraction) of wrong answers will be multiplied by five and deducted from your final score.
  13. The two other hosts must agree that at least one of your picks is “not obvious”, and one of your picks is “pretty cool”.
  14. Every pick has to be related to Apple or the tech industry.
  15. No picking things that the picker of the pick could make come true by themselves or by inducing others, including taking a job at Apple.
  16. No picking things that are not provably true or false. Whether or not you can prove a negative is best left for the philosophers.
  17. No altering the prediction document after the show is over. We all remember what happened in 2020.
  18. After scoring is complete, each host must place the official Pickies Refrigerator Magnets in the proper configuration on their refrigerator for the next year. Winner at the center of the door, second place in the lower left corner, third place in the lower right corner.
  19. The winner of each competition will be given access to the @wwdcshindigking and @pickiesadmiral twitter accounts, as appropriate.

Notes

The official copy of the Charter is on Jason Snell's microblog.

While not currently codified in the Charter, there is an additional rule for the Lightning Round: picks are made in rounds in draft format, so repeats are not allowed, and hosts may drop out at any time (although this is rare). In addition, the number of picks is capped at 5.
